The Data Pipeline: Start to Finish

1

Live Ingestion

The engine securely fetches your live holdings from Groww and pulls 5 years (1,260 days) of adjusted close prices from Yahoo Finance.

2

Volatility Mapping

We calculate daily log returns and build a Covariance Matrix. This maps how volatile each stock is and how they move in relation to one another.

3

Cholesky Sync

We decompose the matrix into a "Cholesky Factor." This ensures that random shocks in the simulation respect the real-world correlations of your portfolio.

4

5,000 Iterations

The engine uses a Student's T-Distribution to run 5,000 simulations. This specifically accounts for "Fat Tails"—the rare but severe market crashes that standard models ignore.

5

Statistical Distillation

We compound all 5,000 paths over 5 years. We then extract the 15th, 50th, and 85th percentiles to find your Bear, Base, and Bull cases.

6

Monthly UI Mapping

Finally, a smoothing algorithm is applied to the paths, and key milestones (12M, 36M, 60M) are flagged for the final mobile-first dashboard.

How to Interpret the Compass

Bear Case

Represents the 15th percentile—an "unfavorable" but realistic market slowdown or correction.

Base Case

The 50th percentile (median). This is the most statistically likely trajectory based on current fundamentals.

Bull Case

The 85th percentile. Represents strong equity rallies and optimistic economic conditions.
